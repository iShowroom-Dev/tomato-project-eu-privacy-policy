# Privacy Policy for Tomato Project EU

**Effective Date:** July 21, 2026

The **Tomato Project EU** mobile application ("Application") was developed by **iShowroom s.r.o.** ("Service Provider", "we", "our", or "us") as an educational and interactive application intended primarily for children, families, schools, museums, and educational institutions.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when the Application is used.

The Application includes educational content, Augmented Reality features, ARUCO marker scanning, interactive characters, localized subtitles, multiple languages, collectible content, and child-friendly mini-games.

---

## Child-Friendly Application

The Application is designed to provide a safe, educational, and age-appropriate experience.

The Application does not include:

* adult or explicit content;
* gambling;
* dating features;
* social networking;
* public user profiles;
* user-generated public content;
* direct messaging or chat;
* voice communication;
* multiplayer functionality;
* targeted advertising; or
* interactions with unknown users.

Users cannot communicate with other users through the Application.

---

## Information We Collect

The Application does not require users to create an account or sign in.

We do not knowingly request or collect personal information such as:

* a child’s full name;
* email address;
* telephone number;
* home address;
* school name;
* precise location;
* photographs;
* voice recordings; or
* contact lists.

The Application does not create public profiles and does not allow children to publish personal information.

---

## Camera and Augmented Reality

The Application may request access to the device’s **camera** to provide Augmented Reality and ARUCO marker-scanning functionality.

The camera may be used to:

* display the real-world environment within the Application;
* recognize supported ARUCO markers;
* start or unlock educational activities;
* display characters or digital objects in Augmented Reality;
* support AR-based mini-games; and
* position digital content in the user’s environment.

Camera access is used only while providing these features.

Images and video from the live camera feed are processed for AR and marker-recognition purposes. They are not collected, uploaded, stored, or shared by iShowroom s.r.o.

The camera is not used for:

* facial recognition;
* identifying children or other individuals;
* behavioral advertising;
* monitoring users;
* creating biometric profiles; or
* recording users without their knowledge.

---

## ARUCO Marker Scanning

The Application may scan predefined visual ARUCO markers placed in museums, exhibitions, schools, printed materials, or other supported locations.

ARUCO markers contain a visual identifier that allows the Application to recognize which activity or content should be displayed.

Scanning an ARUCO marker does not collect personal information about the user.

Marker recognition is used only to:

* identify supported educational content;
* open an activity or mini-game;
* display a character or object;
* track progress within the Application; or
* provide the intended AR experience.

---

## Application Progress and Local Data

The Application may store limited information about the user’s progress, such as:

* completed activities;
* unlocked activities;
* collected digital items;
* selected language;
* subtitle settings;
* game results;
* sound settings; and
* other application preferences.

This information is generally stored locally on the device and is used only to maintain the user’s progress and settings.

It is not used to identify the child and is not made publicly available.

Deleting the Application or clearing its local data may permanently remove saved progress and settings.

---

## Mini-Games and Interactive Content

The Application contains educational and entertainment mini-games that may involve activities such as:

* coloring;
* memory or sequence games;
* puzzles;
* navigation activities;
* object recognition;
* collecting digital items;
* reaction-based games; and
* interactions with animated characters.

Game results and progress are used only to operate the relevant activity, unlock additional content, or display progress within the Application.

The Application does not publish scores, create public leaderboards, or allow users to compare results with unknown users.

---

## Languages, Subtitles, and Localization

The Application provides content in multiple languages and may include subtitles, translated text, localized audio, or localized educational materials.

The selected language and subtitle preferences may be stored locally on the device so that the Application can remember the user’s settings.

Language selection is not used to identify the user or determine their precise location.

## Location Information

The Application does not require or collect precise GPS location information for its standard functionality.

The Application does not track the movements of children or create a location history.

Some external network or platform services may technically process an Internet Protocol address, which can sometimes indicate an approximate geographical area. iShowroom s.r.o. does not use this information to track users or provide location-based advertising.
